✦ About Internyx

A bridge between Moroccan students and the professional world.

Internyx was built to give every student in Morocco — whatever their network, city or background — a fair shot at the internship that will launch their career.

Explore internships → See career paths

Our mission

Opportunity, not luck.

In Morocco, finding a meaningful internship too often depends on who you know. Students from outside Casablanca or Rabat, or without a family network in industry, are left behind — even when their skills are equal or stronger.

Internyx exists to change that. We curate verified internships from companies across the kingdom, score them for quality and fit, and give every student the tools to prepare, apply and grow with confidence.
